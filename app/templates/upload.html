Supported Formats

We support a wide range of image and document formats:

JPG PNG PDF TIFF BMP GIF WEBP

Language Support

Docuflex supports OCR in multiple languages. Select the appropriate language for best results.

Preprocessing

Our preprocessing options enhance OCR accuracy:

  • Grayscale: Converts color images to grayscale
  • Denoise: Removes noise from the image
  • Contrast Enhancement: Improves text visibility
  • Deskew: Straightens tilted images

Privacy First

All document processing is done locally. No files are stored permanently on our servers.
